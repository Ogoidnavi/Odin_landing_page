The Strawhat Pirates

One of the most wanted Pirate crews, these pirates have been wrecking havoc wherever they go and you can help us catch them!

Strawhat Luffy

The captain of the Strawhats, a very dangerous criminal that, together with his crew, declared war against the world government and the son of the revolutionary army's leader, Monkey D. Dragon!

Pirate Hunter Zoro

The Combatant of the Strawhats known as Pirate Hunter Zoro, he is a dangerous swordsman and master of the Santōryū(three sword style), a style created by him. He previously worked as a bounty hunter before joining with Strawhat Luffy.

Cat Burglar Nami

The Navigator of the Strawhats and former member of the Arlong Pirates, known for tricking her opponents with her wits. Do not be fooled by this woman's looks, she is a skilled thief and extremely dangerous when equipped with her staff.

God Usopp

The Sniper of the Strawhats and son of Yasopp of the Red Hair Pirates. In spite of being the weakest in the crew he is extremely deadly with any device capable of shooting projectiles, be it a cannon, artillery, or even a slingshot.

Black Leg Sanji

The Cook of the Strawhats and one of the princes of the Germa Kingdom that worked as a sous chef in the Baratie under Zeff. In spite of being the cook, he is one of the top four fighters in the crew and an extremely versatile one using a kick-based martial art.

Tony Tony Chopper

The pet of the Strawhat Pirates, is a blue nose reindeer native from Drum Island.

Devil Child Nico Robin

The Archaeologist of the Strawhats, the only survivor of the demonic island of Ohara and a researcher of the Poneglyphs. She aims to defy the world government and discover what happened in the void century. Be extremely careful around this woman as she was able to sink six Buster Call warships with her devil fruit powers.

Iron Man Franky

The Shipwright of the Strawhats, known for being extremely strong in terms of physical prowess. He worked as a ship dismantler and then as a bounty hunter, before turning to piracy. Before becoming a cyborg he was able of extraordinary feats of strength, as he managed to disfigure Spandam with a rifle, so be extremely weary of him in close quarters.

Soul King Brook

The Musician of the Strawhats and former captain of the Rumbar Pirates, is a formidable opponent as his skeletal body allows him to perform a variety of acts that would be impossible or fatal for a normal human.

Knight of the Sea Jinbe

The Helmsman of the Strawhats, former captain of the Sun Pirates and former Warlord of the Seas. As you may already know Jinbe is an extremely dangerous pirate able to stand on equal grounds with Fire Fist Ace and, according to our CP0 agents, capable of killing Who's Who a former CP9 agent and rival of Rob Lucci.
